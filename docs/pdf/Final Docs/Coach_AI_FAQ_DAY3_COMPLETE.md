## CATEGORY 1: Getting Started

### Q1: How do I create my first practice?

**Answer:**

Creating your first practice takes just 3 minutes! Here's how:

1. **Create a team** (if you haven't already) - Click "Create Team" and fill in 4 basic fields
2. **Click "⚽ Generate Practice"** in the sidebar
3. **Choose duration** (60, 75, 90, or 120 minutes)
4. **Select session type** - Pick "Balanced" for most practices
5. **Click "Generate Practice"** - Your plan appears in 1-2 seconds!

The practice will include 5-6 drills with warmup, technical work, games, and cool down. You can save it, print it (PDF), or view it on your phone during practice.

**Need step-by-step?** See our [Quick Start Guide](#) for screenshots and detailed instructions.

**Estimated time:** 3 minutes from start to printable practice plan

---

### Q2: Do I need to fill out the entire team profile?

**Answer:**

**No!** You only need 4 basic fields to start generating practices:
- Team name
- Age group
- Skill level
- Roster size

However, completing your profile unlocks smarter practice generation. When you add **formation**, **play style**, and **focus areas** (like "First touch" or "Quick passing"), the AI will prioritize drills that match your team's specific needs. You'll see "Matched focus: First touch" labels on relevant drills.

**Bottom line:** Start with basics, add details later for better results.

**How to complete profile:** Team Hub → Edit Team Profile → Add focus areas

Upgrading takes 10 seconds:

1. **Click "Experience Level"** dropdown in the sidebar (top left)
2. **Select "Advanced"** or "Expert"
3. **New features appear immediately** - no page reload needed

**What you unlock in Expert:**

## CATEGORY 2: Practice Generation

### Q6: How many drills should I include in a 90-minute practice?

**Answer:**

**5-6 drills is optimal** for a 90-minute session. Coach AI automatically selects the right number based on your practice duration:

- **60 minutes:** 4-5 drills
- **75 minutes:** 5 drills
- **90 minutes:** 5-6 drills
- **120 minutes:** 7-8 drills

**Typical 90-minute breakdown:**
- Warmup: 10 minutes
- Technical drill 1: 20 minutes
- Technical drill 2: 20 minutes
- Small-sided game: 30 minutes
- Cool down: 10 minutes

The app ensures drills fit your time perfectly - no rushing or awkward gaps.

**Want more control?** In Advanced/Expert mode, go to Settings → Practice → Default Drill Count to override the automatic selection.

---

### Q7: What's the best session type for pre-match preparation?

**Answer:**

**Use "Game Prep"** session type 1-2 days before a match.

**Why Game Prep is best:**
- Lower physical intensity (saves energy for match)
- Focus on tactical work and set pieces
- Includes match simulation scenarios
- Emphasizes team shape and positioning

**Timing recommendations:**
- **Friday practice before Saturday game:** Game Prep (60-75 min, light intensity)
- **Thursday practice before Saturday game:** Game Prep (75-90 min, medium intensity)

**Other session types:**
- **Balanced:** Regular practice days (Tuesday, Thursday)
- **Technical Focus:** Skill development, early season
- **Fitness:** Conditioning focus, pre-season

**Pro tip:** After a match, use "Technical Focus" for the next practice to work on weaknesses you observed.

---

### Q8: Can I use the same drills in consecutive practices?

**Answer:**

**Yes, you CAN** - but Coach AI helps you avoid it by default.

**Built-in variety management:**
- Drills used in the past 7 days are labeled "Recently Used"
- The AI automatically prioritizes "Fresh" drills (not used recently)
- You'll see variety without thinking about it

**When to repeat drills:**
- Players need more reps to master a skill
- The drill worked really well
- You're building progressive complexity over 2-3 weeks

**How to repeat a drill:**
- Use the "Reuse Practice" button (Practice History → Reuse)
- Star your favorite drills, then generate with "Prioritize Favorites" on
- In Expert mode, create a practice template with specific drills

**Settings control:** Settings → Practice → "Hide Recent Drills" (ON by default)

---

### Q9: What if I don't have enough players for a drill?

**Answer:**

**The app pre-filters drills** to match your roster size! When you create your team and set "Roster Size: 12", Coach AI only shows drills suitable for 12 or fewer players.

**If your roster changes (injuries, absences):**

**Option 1: Update roster size**
- Team Hub → Edit Team Profile → Change Roster Size
- Generate a new practice - drills will match new number

**Option 2: Adapt the drill yourself**
- Most drills include modification notes for different player counts
- Example: "4v4 game can be played as 3v3 with fewer players"

**Option 3: Use drill library filters** (Advanced mode)
- Drills → Filter → Players → Select actual count
- Browse drills that work for today's numbers

**Pro tip:** Set roster size to your typical practice attendance (not total team roster) for best results.

---

### Q10: Can I edit a generated practice?

**Answer:**

**Yes!** You have several editing options:

**1. Swap individual drills:**
- Click "Swap" button on any drill card
- Get a different drill of the same type and duration
- Keeps the practice structure intact

**2. Reorder drills:**
- Click ↑ or ↓ arrows to move drills up or down
- Adjust the practice flow to your preference

**3. Regenerate entire practice:**
- Click "Generate Practice" again with same settings
- Get a completely different set of drills
- Previous practice is saved in history

**4. Create template** (Expert mode):
- Save specific drill sequences as reusable templates
- Modify templates for different sessions

**What you CANNOT do:**
- Edit drill descriptions (they're standardized)
- Change drill duration (would break practice timing)
- Mix drills from different categories incorrectly

**Workaround:** In Expert mode, create custom drills with your modifications.

## CATEGORY 3: Features & Customization

### Q11: How does the AI drill matching work?

**Answer:**

The AI uses **5 factors** to select the perfect drills for YOUR team:

**1. Focus Areas** (from Team Profile)
- You add skills like "First touch" or "Quick passing"
- AI prioritizes drills tagged with those skills
- Shows "Matched focus: First touch" labels

**2. Age & Skill Level**
- Filters out drills too advanced or too simple
- Ensures age-appropriate content

**3. Team Style** (Formation & Play Style)
- Possession-based teams get more passing drills
- Counter-attack teams get transition drills

**4. Recency** (Variety Management)
- Avoids drills used in past 7 days
- Keeps practices fresh automatically

**5. Session Type**
- "Game Prep" = more tactical drills
- "Technical Focus" = more skill drills
- "Balanced" = mix of everything

**The more you fill out your team profile, the smarter the AI becomes!**

**See it in action:** Complete your team profile → Generate practice → Look for "Matched focus" labels

```html
                } else {
                    question.style.display = 'none';
                }
            });
        });
        
        // Accordion toggle
        document.querySelectorAll('.question-header').forEach(function(header) {
            header.addEventListener('click', function() {
                const answer = this.nextElementSibling;
                const icon = this.querySelector('.toggle-icon');
                
                answer.classList.toggle('open');
                icon.classList.toggle('open');
            });
        });
        
        // Back to top button
        window.onscroll = function() {
            if (document.body.scrollTop > 300 || document.documentElement.scrollTop > 300) {
                document.getElementById('backToTop').style.display = 'block';
            } else {
                document.getElementById('backToTop').style.display = 'none';
            }
        };
        
        function scrollToTop() {
            window.scrollTo({top: 0, behavior: 'smooth'});
        }
    </script>
</body>
</html>
```
