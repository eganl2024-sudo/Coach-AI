**3.2** You'll see the Practice Generator page with several options:

> **[SCREENSHOT 5 LOCATION]**  
> **File:** `screenshot-05-practice-generator-configured.png`  
> **What to show:** Practice Generator page with settings configured as shown below  
> **Annotations needed:**  
> - Number each setting area (1-4)  
> - Highlight the "Generate Practice" button in green  
> **Size:** Full width, ~600px height

**3.3** Configure your practice (or use the smart defaults):

**① Date**
- Default: Today's date
- You can change this to plan ahead

**② Duration** 
- Use the slider or click preset buttons
- **Select: 90 minutes** ← Most common practice length
- Options: 60, 75, 90, or 120 minutes

**③ Number of Players**
- **Pre-filled from your team!** (14 in our example)
- This ensures drills are sized correctly

**④ Session Type**
- Select **● Balanced** ← Best for regular practice days
- Other options:
  - ○ Technical Focus (skill development)
  - ○ Game Prep (before matches)
  - ○ Fitness (conditioning focus)

**3.4** Click the big green **"Generate Practice"** button

⏱️ **Wait 1-2 seconds...** The AI is selecting the perfect drills for your team!

## 🚀 NEXT STEPS

Now that you've created your first practice, here's what to explore:

### ✅ Immediate Actions (Recommended)

**□ 1. Complete Your Team Profile** (5 minutes)  
Add focus areas like "First touch" or "Quick passing" so the AI prioritizes relevant drills.
- Navigate to: **Team Hub** → **Edit Team Profile**
- Add 3-5 focus areas your team needs to work on
- Save and generate your next practice to see the difference!

**□ 2. Browse the Drill Library** (10 minutes)  
Explore all available drills and star your favorites.
- Navigate to: **🏀 Drills** in sidebar (Advanced Mode)
- Use filters to find drills by category, intensity, or age
- Click ⭐ to star drills you love
- Starred drills will appear more often in your practices

**□ 3. Explore Practice History** (2 minutes)  
See all your saved practices and learn the "Reuse" shortcut.
- Navigate to: **📅 History** in sidebar
- Click on any past practice to view details
- Try the **"Reuse"** button → generates same drills with new date
- **This is the fastest way to plan:** 1 minute total! 🔥
